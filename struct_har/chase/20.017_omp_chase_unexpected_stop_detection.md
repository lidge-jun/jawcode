## 왜 gap인가

OMP added an unexpected-stop classifier, retry prompts, settings, and a three-retry cap for sessions that stop mid-response unexpectedly. JWC completion discipline forbids fabricating progress or masking failures, so the valuable reference is bounded retry with explicit classification and transparent reporting.
